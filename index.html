Homes for Sale is a Google Action that demonstrates what it would feel like to have a voice-enabled app integrate with nationwide real estate data that follows you everywhere you have access to Google Assistant, which is currently about 500 million different places. Whether you're driving, typing, or in the middle of another conversation, it takes zero install and no more than one simple phrase to get you actionable data toward your next closed deal: "Hey Google, Talk to me about Homes for Sale."
